ChicagoWiki

A wiki I set up using WikkaWiki software for The City in World History, an undergraduate course offered at IIT in Spring of 2012. In coordination with Dr. Maureen Flanagan, the course's instructor, I laid out a basic site structure, and the students did the rest.

The site examines the history of the city of Chicago from the 19th century to present day through four different lenses: Site of Civilization, City as Imaginary, Site of Commerce & Industry, and Site of Tourism.

I also created an instructional guide to using the wiki, which can be found here.
