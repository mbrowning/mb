  1. Way too much going on in this shopping cart. The little arrows next to the links at the bottom aren't instructive, so they amount to visual clutter. The "free delivery" copy doesn't belong here, especially since it’s repeated to the left above the search bar. And the fonts and cart graphic are bulky.
  2. With the login/register link surrounded on all sides, will anyone ever see it?
  3. This is an out-of-place customer service link; it adds to the clutter of this section.
  4. The banner and main content sections right on top of each other make this area of the page too dense; there is no obvious focal point. Both are graphics-heavy and use the same bold font. In this particular banner, the retail packaging for QuickBooks could be cut.
  5. The main content area rotates too quickly. It's on a five-second delay; seven would be less jarring.
  6. This nav has no theme, an image of an employee which suggests live chat but instead leads to an ad, and unusable, contradictory sub-sections. All of its content could be better placed in other areas.
  7. In particular, Shopping Tools contains links to OD-branded products, an A-Z product listing, and “Worklife Rewards”, which is not described anywhere on the page—there’s a lack of a theme even inside this one section. Also, OD-branded products should be given much more prominent placement.
  8. These product columns underneath the main content area are a waste of space and an awkward way to display subcategories.
  9. Too many heavy fonts and graphics in these promo areas; they more or less cancel each other out.
